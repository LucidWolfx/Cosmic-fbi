General Rules

Updated: June 19, 2025

  • Respect all players and staff members. Harassment, hate speech, and toxicity will not be tolerated.
  • Roleplay at all times. This is an RP server, and breaking character without reason disrupts immersion.
  • Use common sense. If something seems against the spirit of the server, avoid doing it.

RDM / VDM

Updated: June 19, 2025

  • RDM (Random Deathmatch): Killing another player without valid RP reason or escalation.
  • VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch): Using a vehicle to run over or attack players without valid RP.

Powergaming

Updated: June 19, 2025

  • Acting outside the bounds of your character or abusing mechanics to gain an unfair advantage.

FailRP

Updated: June 19, 2025

  • FailRP includes unrealistic or immersion-breaking actions such as respawning in combat or ignoring injuries.

Exploiting

Updated: June 19, 2025

  • Using bugs, glitches, or third-party software to exploit the game is strictly prohibited.

Robberies

Updated: June 19, 2025

  • All robberies must be roleplayed thoroughly with reasonable demands and interaction.

Metagaming

Updated: June 19, 2025

  • Using information obtained out-of-character (OOC) to benefit your in-character actions.

Camping Green Zones

Updated: June 19, 2025

  • Do not camp at spawn or green zones to initiate RP or criminal activity. These are safe zones.
